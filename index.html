How Malaysia Moves

From railway networks to daily ridership, this visualisation explores how Malaysia’s public transport system recovered, shifted, and continues to shape everyday mobility.

Public transport demand changed sharply after 2020, but ridership has gradually returned across major services.

Conclusion

Malaysia’s public transport data shows how mobility is shaped by rail infrastructure, urban demand, and changing travel behaviour. The visualisation highlights both the recovery of ridership and the uneven spatial distribution of rail access.
