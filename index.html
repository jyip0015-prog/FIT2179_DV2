💡

Recovery Driven by Rail

Malaysia’s public transport data shows a clear recovery story after major disruption periods. Rail services carry the larger share of ridership, especially through high-demand urban services such as LRT Kelana Jaya and MRT Kajang. Bus services remain important as a supporting mode, but the visualisations suggest that rail is the main driver of Malaysia’s public transport recovery. The next challenge is not only increasing ridership, but also improving accessibility and coverage across different regions.
